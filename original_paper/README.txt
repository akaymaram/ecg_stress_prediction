1. Data_collection_paper.pdf is Jennifer A. Healey's phd thesis, which includes the details of how the 18 samples were collected.

2. msc_dissertation_christopher_ottesen _2017.pdf is Christopher Ottesen's full work, which is also summarized at
https://dataespresso.com/en/2019/01/30/Stress-detection-with-wearable-devices-and-Machine-Learning/

3. dataframe_hrv.csv is the Ottesen's extracted and cleaned dataset, which he uses for training his ML model.

4. dataset_overview.html provides information and description of dataframe_hrv.csv columns.

5. The folder test_data contains the results from testing the trained model in a real-life scenario, and is separated into two subfolders based on the two motion pictures shown to participants.
